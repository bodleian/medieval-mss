All Souls College MS. 37

Physical Description

Secundo Folio: A (fol. 1) quasi abrahei; B sino in rodanum; C summatis autem.
Form: codex
Support: Written on 214 membrane leaves, including two (fols. 95 and 157) of which only stubs remain; preceded by one paper flyleaf (s. xviii) and followed by one medieval binding leaf (*E), one medieval membrane flyleaf, and one paper flyleaf (s. xviii.

Fore- and endleaves: fol. ir, number; fol. iv, blank; fol. 1rv, text, = *E below; fols. IIr—IIIv, blank.

Extent: 214 leaves
Dimensions (leaf): 380 × 245 mm.
Foliation: Leaves numbered in modern pencil.

Collation

Collation: 18 (wants 1) 2-118 128 (stub of 8 = fol. 95) 13-198 208 (wants 7-8; stub of 6 = fol. 157); 218 228 (wants 6-8, blank?) || 23-258 264 (wants 3-4) || 2710 || 288.
